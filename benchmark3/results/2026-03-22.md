## 总结

### 性能排名

**Parse (typed)**:  fastjson3 > wast > fastjson2 >> jackson > gson

**Write (UTF8 bytes)**: fastjson3 >> wast > fastjson2 >> jackson >> gson

### fastjson3 vs 主要对手

| 对比 | Parse 优势 | Write 优势 |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| vs fastjson2 | +28% ~ +125% | +63% ~ +217% |
| vs jackson | +181% ~ +235% | +129% ~ +232% |
| vs gson | +200% ~ +300% | +700% ~ +800% |
| vs wast | ±3% ~ +8% (Parse接近) | +10% ~ +31% |

### JDK 21 vs JDK 25

| Library | JDK 25 相对 JDK 21 |
|---------|-------------------|
| fastjson3 | +5% ~ +13% (小幅提升) |
| fastjson2 | +10% ~ +50% (显著提升) |
| wast | +5% ~ +17% |
| jackson | +2% ~ +10% |

JDK 25 对所有库都有提升，fastjson2 受益最大（可能因为 JVM 优化对反射路径更友好）。

### 架构对比 (JDK 25)

| Benchmark | ARM | x86 | x86/ARM |
|-----------|-----|-----|---------|
| ClientsParse fj3 | 1,348K | 2,937K | **2.18x** |
| ClientsWrite fj3 | 2,118K | 3,780K | **1.78x** |
| UsersWrite fj3 | 2,303K | 4,329K | **1.88x** |

x86 (AMD EPYC 9T95) 在这些测试中约比 ARM 快 1.8-2.2 倍。
